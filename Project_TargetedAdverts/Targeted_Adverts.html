Data Description:

The dataset "airline" encompasses customer satisfaction data related to passengers' experiences on an airline. The data can be accessed here. Here are the key variables included in the dataset:

  • Satisfaction: A binary indicator reflecting overall satisfaction or dissatisfaction with the experience.
  • Gender: Passenger's gender.
  • Customer Type: Passenger's loyalty status.
  • Age: Passenger's age in years.
  • Type of Travel: Purpose of the passenger's travel.
  • Class: Ticket class (Business, Eco, Eco Plus).
  • Flight Distance: Length of the flight (assumed to be in miles).
  • Various Satisfaction Metrics: Likert-scaled satisfaction ratings on aspects such as seat comfort, departure and arrival time convenience, food and drink quality, gate location, inflight wifi service, online support, online booking process, leg room, baggage handling, check-in service, cleanliness, online boarding, onboard service, departure delay, and arrival delay.

Data Analysis Task:

This project involved two goals. Use random forests and/or individual classification trees to perform your analysis.

  1. Identify Some Opportunities to Improve Customer Satisfaction:

  2. Identify the Most Dissatisfied Customers to Target for Coupons:
    • Develop a process to determine which dissatisfied passengers should be offered coupons.
    • Consider the fairness and cost-effectiveness of using certain variables for targeting.
    • Determine criteria for identifying passengers eligible for coupons, considering the binary satisfaction response.
